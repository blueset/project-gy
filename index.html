What is Project Gy?

Project Gy (pronounce as 'Project Gee-Wai'), is a lyric-centered web-log using PHP and MySQL.

Through Project Gy, people can share their thoughts and feelings through lyrics of songs, and share them with all over the world, by simply type into the blog. Furthermore, Project Gy can also genetate typographical pictures, and dynamic images for forum signatures based on the lyric posts.

This project is inspired by my daily sharing of lyrics on social-working websites like Facebook and Twitter. I was then thinking about writing a blog program that is for lyric sharing, and this whole thing came to my mind.

Features

Share your lyrics of thoughts.

Gy keeps all your favorite lyrics online. You can easily share it to the platform you like using RSS and IFTTT.

It's your special diary.

It maybe just a moment, when you are moved by just a few lines in a song. With Gy, you can have it recorded everywhere using your smartphone, tablet or computer.

Visualise it.

Image generator module of helps you to create images for better sense of expressing the emotion. Also, through dynamic picture, the realtime status can be shared to blogs and forums as a widget.

Compose together.

Gy supports multi-user collaboration. By creating accounts, you can collect your favourite lyrics with your friends, record your memories together.
